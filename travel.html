Sebastian inlet surfing

Stunning, natural Sebastian Inlet State Park is arguably the epicenter of East Coast competitive surfing. It's where native sons including Kelly Slater, the Hobgood twins and the Lopez brothers first learned to rip in powerful waves. On the north side of the inlet, swells reflect off the jetty and are amplified.
